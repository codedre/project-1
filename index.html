Introduction

Think you know all of McDonald's Menu offerings? Test your skills with this memory game and the head over to your local McDonald's for a victory snack.

How to play?

  1. Select your challenge difficulty
  2. Click on TWO squares at a time to reveal contents and check for a match
  3. If there is a match the squares will remain flipped
  4. Successfully match all pairs in the table to win!
